## The coverage trap

Teams celebrate when automation coverage crosses 90%. Leadership sees green dashboards. Engineers feel progress.

But coverage alone does not measure whether the right scenarios are automated, whether tests are stable, or whether failures provide actionable signal.

## What to measure instead

- **Risk-based coverage** of critical user journeys
- **Defect escape rate** and mean time to detect
- **Flake rate** and test maintenance cost
- **Pipeline feedback time** from commit to actionable results

## Leadership takeaway

Use coverage as a diagnostic, not a destination. The goal is release confidence — not a percentage on a slide.
